WHO WE ARE

The Georgia Tech Triathlon Team is a student-led organization that encompasses all things multisport at Georgia Tech. We invite new members of all skill levels to participate in everything from a group swim bike run to compete in the highest level of Collegiate racing. Whether you are an experienced triathlete, a complete beginner, or have never even heard of the sport we guarantee you'll get in love with triathlon immediately.

WHAT WE DO

We love Triathlon and we wish we could train all day long, but we are also members of one of the most prestigous and rigorous universities in the nation and we understand the commitment this conveys. That's why we have such a flexible schedule and many options for you to train and succeed at your own pace.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

In the 2014-2015 academic school year, we surpass all our goals! We won the Southeast Collegiate Triathlon Conference (SECTC) Overall Team Standings, and won both the Men’s and Women’s Individual SECTC Championship Titles. We sent over a dozen athletes to the USA Triathlon Collegiate National Championships in the Draft-Legal, Non-drafting, and Mixed-Team Relay events. Amazingly enough most of our Members had never done a Triathlon before joining the Team. Over the past four years, our team has grown exponentially and we wan't to continue growing and we want YOU to be part of it.

Foster the sport of triathlon at Georgia Tech by providing opportunities for students and the Georgia Tech community to learn and participate in the sport through social activities, training, and racing.
