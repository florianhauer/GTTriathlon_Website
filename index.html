WHO WE ARE

The Georgia Tech Triathlon Team is a student-led organization that encompasses all things multisport at Georgia Tech. Our members come from a wide range of skill levels and participate in everything from social swim, bike, and run training to competition at the highest level of collegiate racing. Whether you are an experienced endurance athlete seeking competition, a weekend warrior or beginner searching for other active, like-minded individuals, a novice who has never even heard of the sport, or a supporter interested in helping us pursue our mission, we welcome all to join us as members or support us as partners!

WHAT WE DO

Our team is a diverse group of undergraduate and graduate Georgia Tech students united by our passion for multisport and triathlon. However, our institution’s rigorous and demanding programs require a strong commitment to academics, which we recognize. To accommodate our members’ full schedules, we offer a wide range of group workouts, flexible training plans, and many other options for members to train and succeed at their own pace. We compete in the Southeast Collegiate Triathlon Conference (SECTC) and nationally against other collegiate teams. Additionally, our team members race together at dozens of events annually, and we support members pursuing the sport outside of our conference. Finally, we promote team camaraderie at occasional social events and an annual training camp.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

In the 2014-2015 academic school year, we raced our way to the top of the SECTC Overall Team Standings and won both the Men’s and Women’s Individual SECTC Championship Titles. Additionally, we sent over a dozen athletes to the USA Triathlon Collegiate National Championships in the Draft-Legal, Non-drafting, and Mixed-Team Relay events. Most of our members join the team without ever completing a triathlon beforehand, and each new member that our team brings to the sport is viewed as a meaningful achievement for the team. Our team is always growing and improving, and our team always supports members in accomplishing their individual athletic goals.

OUR MISSION

To foster the sport of triathlon at Georgia Tech by providing opportunities for students and the Georgia Tech community to learn and participate in the sport through social activities, training, and racing.
